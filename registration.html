Applications vastly outnumbered the school capacity, and were of excellent quality. We regret we had to apply a stringent selection.
All candidates will be informed of the decision by an email by 20th April, 2025.

If your participation is confirmed, please register as soon as possible, as we have a long waiting list.

To REGISTER, please navigate to this page on the IVSLA website and proceed with the payment of the fee, which implies that your registration is completed.
On that page, please access the English version and select:

  • Payment in the absence of PagoPA notice
  • Amministrazione in the Beneficiary Unit
  • The Alma Dal Co School 2025 in the Tribute code box

Then follow the procedure.

We strongly recommend securing various bookings as soon as possible, as well.
